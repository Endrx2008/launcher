Master Key System Usage Guide

Learn how to use the Skorpion_Os lite system, with function keys to control your system.

Master Key

Alt

ALT IS THE MASTER KEY

Open Launcher

Alt + F1

Press the Master Key and F1 together to open the launcher.

Close Last Clicked Window

Alt + F5

Press the Master Key and F5 together to close the last window you clicked on.

Launch Firefox

Alt + F7

Press the Master Key and F7 together to launch Firefox browser.

Open Terminal

Alt + F10

Press the Master Key and F10 together to open the terminal.

×
